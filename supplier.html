Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for leads?

expand_more
We use a simple credit system. Top up your wallet, and credits are deducted only when a homeowner requests your specific services based on your matching criteria. Leads cost between £0.50 and £4.00 depending on trade volume.

Is there a commission on jobs?

expand_more
No. We believe you earned the job, so you keep the reward. We are purely a lead-generation platform. Once you have the homeowner's details, the contract is between you and them.

Can I pause my account?

expand_more
Yes. You have complete control. If you are fully booked for the month, simply toggle your profile to 'Inactive' with one click. No leads will be sent, and no credits will be spent.
